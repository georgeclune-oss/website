How Many Airports Are There in the World?

There are 85,377 airstrips and 893 airports worldwide, ranging from major international hubs to small regional and private landing sites. This dataset combines OpenStreetMap aviation data, aeronautical classifications, historical records, and manual verification to ensure accuracy and global coverage.

Organisations / Nations Included

The map includes all recognised civilian, military, commercial, and private aviation facilities. Major operators include national aviation authorities, airport authorities, private companies, and military organisations across every continent.
