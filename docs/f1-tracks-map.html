How Many F1 Tracks Are There?

There are 77 rocket launch facilities across the globe, not all of these are still used in the Grand Prix but they have been at one point. This dataset combines OpenStreetMap data, official Formula One data, and manual verification to ensure accuracy.

Organisations / Nations Included

There are 34 Countries that contain a Formula One Circuit. Some tracks are no longer used in the Grand Prix (e.g. Formula One terminated relations with Russia after the war in Ukraine).
