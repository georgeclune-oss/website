How Many Spaceports Are There in the World?

There are 86 rocket launch facilities across the globe, including orbital, sub‑orbital, research, and experimental launch facilities. This dataset combines OpenStreetMap data, aerospace classifications, historical records, and manual verification to ensure accuracy.

Organisations / Nations Included

The map includes all organisational and national space launch facilities. Organisations include SpaceX, Blue Origin, Rocket Lab, and others. National agencies include NASA, Roscosmos, CNSA, JAXA, ESA, ISRO, and more.
