How Many Castles Are in Ireland?

Ireland has over 1,620 known church buildings across 32 counties. Castles first started to be built in Ireland in the 12th century after the invasion of the Normans. The dataset combines OpenStreetMap data, historical records, and manual verification to ensure accuracy.

Church Denominations Included

The map includes all major castles in Ireland. There are many castles in Ireland. The majority of these castles are abandoned or in ruins but many are still in use as hotels or private homes.
